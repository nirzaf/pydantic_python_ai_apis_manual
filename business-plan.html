BUSINESS PLAN

Labneh & Co.

A Premier Cheese-Focused Restaurant in Doha, Qatar

Prepared: April 2025

Confidential Document

1. Executive Summary

Labneh & Co. will be a unique dining concept in Doha featuring cheese-based dishes complemented by premium chicken and beef options. The restaurant will blend traditional Arabic cheese preparations with international fusion elements to create a distinctive culinary experience that appeals to both locals and expatriates in Qatar's diverse dining scene.

Business Opportunity

The restaurant will capitalize on the growing trend of experiential dining in Qatar, where customers seek immersive culinary experiences that highlight both local heritage and global influences. With its strategic focus on high-quality ingredients and innovative cheese-based menu items, Labneh & Co. is positioned to capture a profitable share of Doha's expanding restaurant market, which is projected to grow at a CAGR of 9.11% to reach USD 3.09 billion by 2030.

Our projected initial investment amounts to QAR 3,200,000 (updated from finance section) with an expected breakeven point within 18-24 months of operation. The business aims to achieve a return on investment of 25-30% by the end of Year 3.

2. Business Concept & USP

Concept

Labneh & Co. will be a modern, upscale casual dining restaurant that celebrates cheese in all its forms, with particular emphasis on Middle Eastern cheese varieties and their creative fusion with international cuisines. The restaurant will feature an open kitchen concept where guests can observe the cheese-making process for certain dishes, creating an interactive dining experience.

Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

  • First dedicated cheese-focused restaurant in Doha with both traditional and fusion offerings
  • In-house cheese production for select items, ensuring freshness and uniqueness
  • Interactive dining experience including potential cheese-tasting flights and visible preparation
  • Menu that respects local culinary traditions while offering innovative interpretations
  • Premium halal meat selections carefully chosen to complement the cheese-focused dishes

3. Market Analysis

Target Market Demographics

Our target audience includes affluent Qatari nationals, diverse expatriate communities (Western, Arab, Asian), and tourists seeking unique culinary experiences. We aim for a broad appeal across age groups (25-65) interested in quality dining.

Customer Segmentation (Illustrative)

Market Trends in Qatar's Restaurant Industry

  • Rise of experiential dining combining local flavors with global influences
  • Growing interest in traditional foods presented with modern aesthetics
  • Increasing demand for premium casual dining options offering value and quality
  • Rising food awareness and sophistication among consumers seeking unique concepts
  • Digital integration in ordering, reservations, and marketing
  • Emphasis on locally sourced ingredients and transparent supply chains

Competitive Analysis

The restaurant landscape in Doha is vibrant but lacks a dedicated, upscale cheese-focused concept. Key competitors include high-end hotel restaurants, established Middle Eastern fine dining, and popular international casual chains. Labneh & Co.'s niche focus, quality ingredients, and blend of tradition/innovation provide a strong competitive edge.

6. Operational Plan

Staffing Structure & Requirements

A skilled team is crucial. Key roles include General Manager, Executive Chef (with cheese expertise), Restaurant Manager, Sous Chef, Pastry Chef, Service Staff, Kitchen Crew, and Marketing support.

Organizational Structure

Key Operational Processes

  • Supply Chain: Establish relationships with reliable local suppliers for produce/meats and reputable importers for specialty cheeses. Inventory management system (e.g., POS integrated) is essential.
  • Kitchen Workflow: Standardized recipes, batch preparation for base sauces/components, Ã  la minute finishing for freshness. Clear sections for hot, cold, pastry, and cheese prep.
  • Service Standards: Emphasis on knowledgeable staff (menu, cheese types), efficient order taking (digital POS), table management, and creating a welcoming atmosphere. Regular training.
  • Quality Control: Daily checks by chefs, regular tasting, monitoring customer feedback, adherence to hygiene standards (HACCP principles).

Legal & Compliance (Qatar Specific)

Ensuring full compliance with Qatari regulations is paramount.

Business Registration Establish LLC (potentially with Qatari partner), obtain Commercial Registration (CR), Trade License, Tax Card.
Ministry Approvals Ministry of Commerce and Industry (MOCI), Ministry of Municipality (Baladiya) for food establishment license, Civil Defence for safety, potentially Ministry of Public Health (MoPH).
Staffing Compliance Obtain necessary visas/residence permits, health certificates for all food handlers (renewed periodically), potentially specific food safety certifications.
Premises Lease agreement compliance, building permits for renovations, adherence to zoning laws, Civil Defence inspection and approval.
Ongoing Annual renewal of licenses, regular health/safety inspections, tax compliance, adherence to labor laws.

  4. Phase 4: Ongoing Marketing: Regular content updates, seasonal menu promotions, targeted campaigns, continuous engagement with customers online and offline.

9. Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Identifying potential risks and planning mitigation strategies is crucial for success.

Risk Factor Potential Impact Mitigation Strategy
Intense Competition Difficulty attracting customers, price pressure. Unique cheese-focused USP, exceptional quality & service, strong branding, loyalty programs, targeted marketing.
Staffing Challenges Difficulty recruiting/retaining skilled staff (esp. cheese knowledge), high turnover costs. Competitive compensation, positive work environment, robust training programs (internal & external), clear career paths, potentially sourcing specialized international talent.
Supply Chain Disruptions Inconsistent quality/availability of key ingredients (esp. imported cheeses), price volatility. Develop relationships with multiple suppliers, diversify menu slightly to allow flexibility, in-house production of core items (labneh), strong inventory management.
Economic Downturn Reduced consumer discretionary spending affecting high-end dining. Offer varied price points within the menu, introduce value-driven lunch specials or set menus, focus on exceptional value proposition.
Regulatory Changes New health codes, licensing requirements, labor laws increasing costs or complexity. Stay informed via industry associations, engage local legal/consulting expertise, maintain buffer for compliance costs, proactive adherence to standards.
Food Safety Incidents Damage to reputation, legal action, closure. Strict adherence to HACCP and local hygiene standards, rigorous staff training, quality checks on suppliers, transparent processes.

Proactive Risk Management

We will implement a continuous risk monitoring process involving regular management reviews, analysis of financial performance and customer feedback, and staying updated on market trends and regulations. Maintaining adequate financial reserves and contingency plans for key risks (e.g., equipment failure, key staff departure) will be prioritized.

10. Conclusion

Labneh & Co. presents a compelling opportunity to introduce a unique and sophisticated cheese-focused dining experience to Doha's dynamic F&B market. By blending authentic Middle Eastern cheese traditions with international culinary innovation, complemented by premium meat options, the restaurant is strategically positioned to appeal to a diverse clientele of locals, expatriates, and tourists.

The concept capitalizes on key market trends favoring experiential dining, quality ingredients, and unique culinary narratives. While challenges exist within the competitive landscape, our distinct USP, strong operational plan, targeted marketing, and experienced management approach provide a solid foundation for success.

Path to Success

  • Unwavering Focus on Quality: From ingredients to service execution.
  • Authenticity & Innovation: Balancing heritage with modern appeal.
  • Strong Brand Storytelling: Communicating the unique concept effectively.
  • Operational Excellence: Efficient systems, cost control, and consistent delivery.
  • Customer Centricity: Listening to feedback and building loyalty.
  • Adaptability: Responding effectively to market changes and challenges.

With the planned investment and strategic execution outlined in this document, Labneh & Co. is poised to become a profitable and highly regarded culinary landmark in Doha, Qatar.
